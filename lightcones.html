What are Light Cone's

Light Cones are Garden of Recollection technology. Created from refined memory fragments, they can contain not only memories, but also experiences and abilities. Because of this, they’re under heavy restriction by the Interastral Peace Corporation

Best Light Cones

Light Cones are items that can be equipped by characters to boost their Base HP, Base ATK, and Base DEF and grant a passive ability to characters whose Path matches that of the Light Cone.

All Light Cones have a rarity ranging between 3-star to 5-star, and Light Cones with a higher rarity have higher base stats.

Ways of obtaining Light Cones include, but are not limited to, completing quests, completing reward milestones, opening chests, and Warps.
